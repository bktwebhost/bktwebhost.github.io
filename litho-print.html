Rethink traditional litho print

We are able to offer an extensive range of high quality printed documents ultilising our litho printing and managed web facilities. Using the latest technology, our print machinery provides us with the ability to produce high quality full colour print on a range of stocks using a variety of unique finishes.

We fulfill our client’s requirements through a seamless service from concept and design of printed communications through to despatch and mail out. We support all our processes with 24-hour facilities and each client has a dedicated client relationship manager as part of the account team.

BKT’s Colour Management complies with ISO 12647-2. Using Bodoni Press-sign our projects are measured and monitored to ensure consistency. Our cloud based Colour Management allows clients to have access to the Quality Monitoring tool throughout a print run.

All of this is complemented by our extensive in-house finishing capabilities, we are able to keep your production requirements under one roof giving maximum control and brand policing of your project.

BKT’s production Team have a wealth of experience in all areas of print and direct mail. Our flexibility and our ability to cater for high-end bespoke print is what really sets us apart from the crowd.

BKT have been producing high quality print for nearly 200 years and currently supply & produce exceptional print to some of the most prestigious brands in the country.

For more information, or to create a Litho Printing Solution that works for you, please get in touch and ask to speak to one of our friendly client services team.

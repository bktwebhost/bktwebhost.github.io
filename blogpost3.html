DIGITAL GOLD INK: THE EVOLUTION OF DIGITAL PRINT

Posted at 31 May, 2019

Gold ink, embossing effects and spot glossing were once the domain of litho printers and additional finishing machines. We have invested in a digital press that offers a diversity of special effects and finishes of the litho presses but with much smaller runs, so you can really impress with small marketing print runs.

Create images that sparkle with Digital Gold Ink – Our gold solution provides a gold metallic effect, ideal for enhancing text, borders, vector art and 4-colour images. Expand business opportunities by adding sparkle and metallic effects to your digital printing applications.

Print you can feel – Our digital colour press’ intelligent dimensional solution gives specified text and images a raised or 3D effect that stands out. Dimensional coating takes place inline, maintaining press productivity while creating high-impact results that grab a reader’s attention.

Print Digital Gold Ink on a large sheet size – You can have these effects printed on up to an 8pp A4 Portrait and 6pp A4 Landscapes on digital runs.

Contact us today to find out more and to get a feel for our full range of digital printing techniques and capabilities.

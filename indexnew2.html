About Us

Since opening our doors almost 200 years ago, Brown Knight & Truscott have continually invested in technology that has placed us at the forefront of the printing world. In today’s digital marketplace, our commitment to innovation has not changed.

Embracing the need to offer a complete print marketing solution across both digital and printed mediums, for clients large and small.

We can help you reach your target audience in one seamless operation from concept to delivery.

Our wealth of experience and expertise combined with the latest technology in online and offline marketing ensures that we can deliver an exceptional range of Design, Print, Direct Mail, Data Management, Integrated Marketing, Fulfilment and Logistics utilising customised Online Print Solutions.

Our customer base is made up from a range of sectors including Financial, Motor, Educational, Charity and Telecommunications to Galleries, Museums, High End Heritage and Retail sites. With experience and expertise in so many sectors, we can help you achieve your marketing goals.

1791

Charles Knight, born in 1791, was the founder of Charles Knight & Co. An author, editor, printer and publisher, his contributions to the literature of the Victorian era were prolific. He also had notable influence on printing development, including the introduction of printing from colour blocks on a hand press, patented in 1838.

1825

William Brown and his partners W & JP Royston established a printing company in Old Broad Street, London. It was the first printer to introduce the fugitive printing of cheques. At this time, a young James Truscott came to London and started a small business expanding first to Great Surrey Street and later to Fleet Street.

The Merger

The merger of these three companies was a pioneering move for the time. The new company was a large operation with two factories in London, the Dowgate works in Tonbridge and the William Lewis factory in Cardiff. Together with several subsidiary businesses the full operation employed over 1,500 people.

Through Adversity

Following an air raid in 1941, in which the London factory was totally destroyed, the Tonbridge factory was extended and modernised becoming the sole production site. The subject of a Management Buy Out in 1993 was the catalyst that changed the focus to a become a provider of complete marketing solutions.

BKT Today

Today Brown Knight & Truscott has the flexibility to offer creative solutions, develop and implement print and facilities management, and manage direct mail campaigns, fully supported by a complete fulfilment and logistics service.

Our Future

We have adapted our offering to meet the needs of the modern customer, and by including digital design and online marketing solutions as part of what we do, we have laid the foundations for future growth and development.

Eco-Friendly Print

We’re committed to environmentally friendly printing and are proud to be certified with ISO 14001, the Forest Stewardship Council (FSC®) and the Programme for the Endorsement of Forest Certification (PEFC).

We believe we have a duty to our staff, clients and suppliers to provide a service that minimises the impact on the environment and our surroundings in general. Our Environment Policy is an extremely simple one: minimise waste and pollution, dispose of waste correctly and recycle wherever possible.

    Our Policy
  1. Reducing and recycling all waste materials including the separating of part-printed and off cut white paper.
  2. Using chemistry-free technology and environmentally friendly materials.
  3. Actively lowering energy consumption and reducing our transport pollution.
  4. Training employees on environmental issues.
  5. Promoting recyclable and renewable materials.
  6. Using vegetable based inks and water based coatings from environmentally friendly producers.
  7. Participating in industry specific and environmental improvement initiatives.
  8. Endorsing sustainable forestry through our paper purchasing policy.
  9. Only using essential resources in order to produce less waste.
  10. Employing only certified waste carriers and disposal sites.

If you would like to find a Printing Solution that minimises your Environmental Impact, learn more about our environmental credentials, or have your printed products display the FSC or PEFC logo, get in touch with our friendly client services team today.

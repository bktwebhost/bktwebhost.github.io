  • Gold and Dimensional Ink

Create images that sparkle with digital gold

Gold ink, embossing effects and spot glossing were once the domain of litho printers and additional finishing machines, not cost effective for small run print.

With our state of the art digital press we can offer these special effects and finishes so you can really impress with small marketing print runs.

Our Gold Solution provides a beautiful rich metallic effect, ideal for enhancing text, borders, vector art and 4-colour images.

It can be used as solid gold giving a similar effect to a traditional foil or may be combined with other CMYK colours to make amazing overall sparkling colour metallic effects.

Expand business opportunities by adding sparkle and metallic effects to your digital printing applications.

It’s a really cost effective enhancement to add gold to low volume orders and will really make your marketing message stand out.

Our digital colour press’ Intelligent Dimensional Solution gives specified text and images a raised or 3D effect that gives your printed marketing the ‘wow’ factor.

Take your print to a new level Print you can really feel and makes your message stand out in a crowded market place. Capture your customer’s attention from the minute they pick it up.

Contact us today for more information about our Digital Print Solutions, or for a copy of our Gold Colour Guide or Dimensional Brochure, to see and feel the effects for yourself.

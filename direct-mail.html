Target individual customers

Communicate directly with each individual customer. Completely control your message – when it’s delivered, what is delivered and exactly who it’s delivered to.

At Brown Knight and Truscott we can create a bespoke Direct Mail printing platform with customised formats and individualised messaging to get your direct mail noticed.

With our experienced approach to data management and security as well as print production, variable data and imaging, your messages will become more targeted and effective. Use data-driven marketing to move beyond the traditional one size fits all into true one-to-one individualisation.

We offer various design, printing and finishing techniques to help you create cost effective, creative, targeted Direct Mail campaigns that appeal to your customers.

Our services cover all elements of data processing, personalisation, enclosing, finishing, despatch, logistics and data capture. We can manage your mailing to anywhere in the UK and worldwide.

We have partnerships with many carriers to help deliver your mail on quick turnaround times, at the most cost effective option. All mail fulfilment is done in-house using the latest automated technology, and a highly skilled team for projects that require hand finishing.

Contact one of our friendly client services team today for more details or to create a Direct Mail Solution that works for you.
